## Fact-Check Lens Analysis

Systematically verify the factual claims in this article against known Johor election facts.

Focus on:
1. **Enumerate claims** — List every verifiable factual claim in the article (statistics, dates, seat counts, candidate names, vote percentages, policy details).
2. **Verify against known data** — Cross-check each claim against:
   - GE14/GE15 official SPR results
   - 2022 Johor state election results (BN: 40/56, PH: 12/56, PN: 4/56)
   - Constituency boundaries (Parlimen P140–P165, DUN N01–N56)
   - Known party leadership and candidate declarations
3. **Flag unverified statistics** — Identify any figures that would require SPR, DOSM, or academic sources to confirm or deny.
4. **Election law compliance** — Note any claims that may conflict with SPR campaign regulations, Election Offences Act, or Polling and Counting regulations.
5. **Contradiction with wiki** — Flag any direct contradictions with the wiki knowledge base.

Return a JSON object with exactly these top-level keys — no wrapper, no preamble:
- `verified_claims`: list of strings, each a claim confirmed accurate (empty list if none)
- `unverified_claims`: list of strings, each a claim that cannot be confirmed without additional sources (empty list if none)
- `false_or_misleading`: list of strings, each an incorrect claim with a brief explanation (empty list if none)
- `flags`: integer count of significant accuracy concerns (0 if none)
- `summary`: string, 2–3 sentences on overall factual integrity of the article
