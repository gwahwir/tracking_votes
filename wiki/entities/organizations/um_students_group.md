# UM Students’ Group (University of Malaya)

## Overview

- **Type:** Student organization at the University of Malaya (UM), Malaysia.
- **Focus:** Advocacy for academic freedom, anti‑corruption awareness, and the right to peaceful assembly among university students.

## Recent Events

- On **21 April 2026**, the UM students’ group reported that police **halted an anti‑graft forum** it had organized, alleging that the authorities suppressed the students’ right to assemble and advocate for academic freedom.  The incident sparked criticism from the student body and civil‑rights observers.  The group expressed regret and claimed the police intervention violated their right to peaceful assembly.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 2026-04-21]
- On **21 April 2026**, the UM students’ group (referred to as “Umany” in the Bahasa‑Malaysia coverage) **expressed regret (kesal)**, stating that the police blocked the anti‑corruption forum and thereby curtailed students’ right to gather and fight for academic freedom.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 2026-04-21]
