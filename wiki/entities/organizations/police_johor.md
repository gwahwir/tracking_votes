# Police Johor – Royal Malaysia Police, Johor State

## Overview

- **Type:** State law‑enforcement agency under the Royal Malaysia Police, responsible for maintaining public order, safety, and enforcing the law in Johor, Malaysia.

## Recent Events
- On **24 April 2026**, Police Johor crippled a drug‑distribution syndicate, seizing **RM 1.8 million** worth of drugs during a raid **on an apartment in Johor Bahru**, arresting **four suspects**, including the **mastermind**, two other individuals, and his **22‑year‑old girlfriend**. [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 24 April 2026, URL: https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/nation/2026/04/24/johor-cops-cripple-syndicate-seize-rm1-8mil-in-drugs]
- On **24 April 2026**, Police Johor reported a shooting incident that was likely sparked by a debt dispute. The suspect, a **71‑year‑old man**, is believed to have lent **RM50,000** in stages to one of the victims, leading to the confrontation. [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 24 April 2026]
