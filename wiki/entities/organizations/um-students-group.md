# UM Students’ Group – University of Malaya

## Overview

A student organization at the University of Malaya (UM) that advocates for academic freedom, transparency and anti‑corruption. In April 2026 the group organised an anti‑graft forum open to the public and the university community. 
[Source: Free Malaysia Today, 21 April 2026, URL: https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/bahasa/tempatan/2026/04/21/kumpulan-pelajar-um-kesal-dakwa-polis-sekat-forum-antirasuah]

## 2026 Incident

- **Alleged police disruption** – On **21 April 2026**, the group reported that police intervened and halted the anti‑graft forum, alleging suppression of the right to assemble and to discuss governance issues. Police said the gathering could potentially disturb public peace and contravene the Universities and University Colleges Act.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 21 April 2026, URL: https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/bahasa/tempatan/2026/04/21/kumpulan-pelajar-um-kesal-dakwa-polis-sekat-forum-antirasuah]
- **Group response** – The organisation issued a statement condemning the action as an infringement on students’ rights and called for accountability from law‑enforcement agencies.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 21 April 2026, URL: https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/bahasa/tempatan/2026/04/21/kumpulan-pelajar-um-kesal-dakwa-polis-sekat-forum-antirasuah]
- **Additional coverage (Bahasa)** – The same incident was covered in Bahasa Malay by Free Malaysia Today on 21 April 2026.

- **UM Students’ Group claim** – The group asserted that police interference curtailed students’ right to assemble and their struggle for academic freedom.
