# UMANY — University of Malaya Students’ Group

## Overview

UMANY, also known as the **UM Students’ Group**, is a student‑led organization at the University of Malaya (UM) that advocates for academic freedom, transparency and anti‑corruption initiatives. It organizes forums, workshops and campaigns on campus. 
[Source: Free Malaysia Today, 21 April 2026, URL: https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/bahasa/tempatan/2026/04/21/kumpulan-pelajar-um-kesal-dakwa-polis-sekat-forum-antirasuah]

## 2026 Incident

- **Police disruption of anti‑graft forum** – On **21 April 2026**, UMANY reported that police intervened and halted an anti‑graft forum it had organised at the University of Malaya, alleging the event violated public order. Police stated the gathering could potentially disturb public peace and contravene the Universities and University Colleges Act. The group claimed the action suppressed students’ right to assemble and undermined academic freedom.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 21 April 2026, URL: https://www.freemalaysiatoday.com/category/bahasa/tempatan/2026/04/21/kumpulan-pelajar-um-kesal-dakwa-polis-sekat-forum-antirasuah]
- **UMANY statement** – The organisation issued a statement condemning the police action and calling for the protection of civil liberties for students and academics.
