# NGO Antara

## Overview

- **Type:** Non‑governmental organization (NGO) operating in Malaysia, involved in zakat collection and distribution.

## Recent Events

- On **21 April 2026**, **three individuals**, including the **deputy chairman**, were **nabbed** by authorities on suspicion of **siphoning RM230 million** in zakat funds.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 2026-04-21]
- On **21 April 2026**, the **deputy chairman** of NGO Antara was **detained** by authorities on suspicion of **misappropriating RM230 million** of zakat funds. Investigations revealed that he had transferred the funds into his **company’s account** for **investment and personal use**.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 2026-04-21]
- The Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission (MACC) also seized **18 luxury vehicles**, including **Porsches** and a **Mercedes‑Benz**, and **properties valued at RM11 million** linked to the case.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 2026-04-21]
