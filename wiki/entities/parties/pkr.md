# Parti Keadilan Rakyat (PKR) — People's Justice Party

## Overview

Parti Keadilan Rakyat (PKR) is a centre‑left political party in Malaysia, founded in 2003. It is a key component of the Pakatan Harapan (PH) coalition and promotes social justice, multiracialism, and institutional reform. [Source: Wikipedia, 2024]

## Ideology

- **Progressive liberalism** – Emphasis on human rights, gender equality, and minority protections.
- **Economic reform** – Calls for fair taxation, reduction of economic inequality, and support for small‑and‑medium enterprises.
- **Good governance** – Anti‑corruption measures, transparency, and accountability.
