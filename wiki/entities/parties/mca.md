# Malaysian Chinese Association (MCA)

## Overview

- **Founded:** 1949
- **Ideology:** Chinese community interests, moderate conservatism
- **Coalition:** Barisan Nasional (BN)

## 2026 Development

- **24 April 2026** – MCA announced **it is seeking younger candidates “who can talk policy, not just rhetoric,”** reflecting a shift toward emphasizing candidate credibility and personality over party loyalty. The statement was made by Andy Teoh, MCA’s youth wing information chief, who noted that voters now gauge credibility and personality rather than party affiliation.
