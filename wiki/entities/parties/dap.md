# Democratic Action Party (DAP) — Democratic Action Party

## Overview

The Democratic Action Party (DAP) is a centre‑left, multiracial political party in Malaysia. Founded in 1965, it is a component of the Pakatan Harapan (PH) coalition and advocates for social justice …

- **Social democracy** – Emphasis on welfare, public services, and equitable wealth distribution.
