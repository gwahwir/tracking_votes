# PAS – Parti Islam Se-Malaysia (PAS)

## Overview
- **Full name:** Parti Islam Se-Malaysia (PAS)
- **Ideology:** Islamist, conservative
- **Position:** Opposition (as of 2026)
- **Key figures:** Mahfodz Mohamed (Johor PAS Commissioner)

## 2026 Activity
- On **27 April 2026**, **Mahfodz Mohamed**, the Johor PAS commissioner, announced that the party is **strengthening its machinery early** to prepare for a **more intense political contest in Muar** ahead of **GE16**. [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 27 April 2026]
