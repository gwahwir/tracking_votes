# Malaysian United Indigenous Party (Bersatu / PPBM)

## Overview

**Parti Pribumi Bersatu Malaysia (PPBM)** — Malaysian United Indigenous Party, commonly known as **Bersatu** — is a Malay nationalist party founded on 7 September 2016 by **Muhyiddin Yassin** and former Prime Minister **Mahathir Mohamad**, both of whom had left UMNO in opposition to then-PM Najib Razak amid the 1MDB scandal. [Source: Wikipedia – Malaysian United Indigenous Party, 2024]

Bersatu is now led by Muhyiddin Yassin and serves as the anchor party of the **Perikatan Nasional (PN)** coalition.
