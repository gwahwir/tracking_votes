# Barisan Nasional (BN) / United Malays National Organisation (UMNO)

## Overview

**Barisan Nasional (BN)** — National Front — is a multi-ethnic conservative coalition that governed Malaysia federally from 1974 until its historic defeat in GE14 (2018). It is the successor to the Alliance Party formed after the 13 May 1969 racial riots. [Source: Wikipedia – Barisan Nasional, 2024]

**United Malays National Organisation (UMNO)** is BN's anchor and largest component party. Founded on 11 May 1946 by Onn Jaafar, UMNO is Malaysia's oldest national party. It was declared unlawful in 1988 and re-registered as UMNO Baru on 13 February 1988. [Source: Wikipedia – United Malays National Organisation, 2024]

---

## Ideology

- **Ketuanan Melayu** (Malay supremacy / Malay primacy) — defends Bumiputera privileges enshrined in the Federal Constitution
- National conservatism and social conservatism
- Defends Islam's constitutional position as the religion of the federation
- Communal representation model: BN comprises separate parties for Malays (UMNO), Chinese (MCA), and Indians (MIC)

[Source: Wikipedia – United Malays National Organisation, 2024]

## Coalition Membership

BN was Malaysia's ruling coalition for 44 years (1974–2018). After the worst result in its history in GE15 (2022), BN joined a Pakatan Harapan-led **National Unity Government**, with UMNO accepting the Deputy Prime Minister role. This represented a historic reversal — BN and PH were bitter rivals in GE14. [Source: Wikipedia – Barisan Nasional, 2024]
