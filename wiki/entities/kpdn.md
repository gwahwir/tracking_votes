# KPDN — Ministry of Domestic Trade and Consumer Affairs (Malaysia)

## Overview

- **Type:** Federal government ministry responsible for domestic trade, consumer protection, price monitoring, and regulation of goods and services in Malaysia.

## 2026 Incident

- **Call to 10 companies for price‑increase justification** – On **24 April 2026**, KPDN summoned ten companies and requested that they submit complete information explaining the recent price hikes they had implemented, aiming to ensure transparency and protect consumers.
