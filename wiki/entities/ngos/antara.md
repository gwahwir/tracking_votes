# Antara NGO — Antara Non‑Governmental Organization

## Overview

Antara NGO is a non‑governmental organization operating in Johor, Malaysia. The organisation is involved in community development and charitable activities, including the collection and distribution of zakat funds.  
*The specific mission and programmes of Antara NGO are not detailed in the source article.*

---

## Recent Controversy (2026)

- **Alleged Misappropriation of Zakat Funds** – An investigation revealed that the deputy chairman transferred **RM230 million** of zakat donations into accounts belonging to his personal company for investment and personal use.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 2026‑04‑21]

- **Detention of Deputy Chairman** – On **21 April 2026**, the deputy chairman of Antara NGO was detained by police in connection with the alleged diversion of the zakat funds.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 2026‑04‑21]

- **Arrests of Three Individuals** – On **21 April 2026**, three persons, including the deputy chairman of Antara NGO, were taken into police custody for alleged involvement in the RM230 million zakat fund misappropriation.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 2026‑04‑21]

- **Three nabbed for allegedly siphoning RM230 million in zakat funds** – The same day, three individuals were arrested for allegedly siphoning the RM230 million zakat donations.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 2026‑04‑21]

- **MACC seizure of assets** – The Malaysian Anti‑Corruption Commission (MACC) seized 18 luxury vehicles, including Porsches and a Mercedes‑Benz, and properties valued at **RM11 million** linked to the alleged misappropriation.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 2026‑04‑21]
