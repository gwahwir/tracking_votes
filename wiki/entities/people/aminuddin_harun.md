# Aminuddin Harun

## Overview

- **Position:** Menteri Besar (Chief Minister) of Negeri Sembilan, member of Parti Keadilan Rakyat (PKR) within the Pakatan Harapan (PH) coalition.

## Recent Statements

- On **27 April 2026**, Aminuddin said that the decision on ties between Pakatan Harapan (PH) and Barisan Nasional (BN) is up to the PH leadership. He also emphasized that his focus is on continuing his duties to the state and governing well. [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 27 April 2026]
- On **27 April 2026**, UMNO withdrew its support for Aminuddin’s administration, prompting an emergency meeting and contributing to a broader political crisis that also involved four chieftains seeking to remove the Yang di‑Pertuan Besar of Negeri Sembilan. [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 27 April 2026]
