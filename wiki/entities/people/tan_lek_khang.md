# Tan Lek Khang – Malaysian Politician

## Overview

- **Full name:** Tan Lek Khang
- **Party:** Parti Pribumi Bersatu Malaysia (Bersatu)
- **Former role:** Leader of the Johor state wing of Bersatu (until 2026)
- **Incident (2026):** On **28 April 2026**, Tan Lek Khang was injured in an accidental self‑inflicted shooting while undergoing firearms training at a shooting range in Kota Tinggi, Johor. He subsequently underwent surgery. [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 28 April 2026]

## 2026 Development

- **Accidental shooting:** Tan Lek Khang is believed to have shot himself during a firearms training session. He was taken to hospital and underwent surgery. [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 28 April 2026]
