# Azmin Ali – Malaysian Politician

## Overview

- **Full name:** Mohamed Azmin bin Ali
- **Party:** Parti Pribumi Bersatu Malaysia (Bersatu)
- **Current roles:**
  - Secretary-General of Bersatu (since November 2024)
  - State Opposition Leader in the Selangor State Assembly (since September 2023)
  - Head of Perikatan Nasional (PN) in Selangor
- **Former roles:**
  - Secretary-General of Perikatan Nasional (PN) — resigned effective 1 January 2026
  - Menteri Besar (Chief Minister) of Selangor (2014–2018)

## 2026 Development

- On **26 April 2026**, Bersatu party president **Muhyiddin Yassin** confirmed that **Azmin Ali** is the **current opposition leader in the Selangor State Assembly** and head of PN in Selangor. [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 26 April 2026]
