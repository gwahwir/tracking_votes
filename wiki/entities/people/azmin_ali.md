# Azmin Ali – Malaysian Politician

## Overview

- **Full name:** Mohamed Azmin bin Ali
- **Party:** Parti Pribumi Bersatu Malaysia (Bersatu)
- **Current roles:
  - Secretary‑General of Bersatu
  - Being mooted **as head of Perikatan Nasional (PN) in Selangor** (2026) [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 26 April 2026]
  - **Opposition Leader in the Selangor State Assembly** (as of April 2026) – stated by Bersatu party president Muhyiddin Yassin [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 26 April 2026]
- **Former role:** Menteri Besar (Chief Minister) of Selangor (2014–2018)

## 2026 Development

- On **26 April 2026**, Bersatu party president **Muhyiddin Yassin** said that **Azmin Ali** is the **current opposition leader in the Selangor State Assembly** and is being **mooted as head of Perikatan Nasional (PN) in Selangor**. [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 26 April 2026]
