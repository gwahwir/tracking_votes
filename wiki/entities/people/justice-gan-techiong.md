# Justice Gan Techiong

## Overview

Justice **Gan Techiong** was appointed as a **High Court judge** in Malaysia on **21 April 2026**. He is the son of **lawyer Gan Kim Sing** and his wife **Tiong Choo**, who travelled from **Johor Bahru** to attend his oath‑taking ceremony in Putrajaya.

## Appointment Details

- **Position:** High Court Judge (Justice)
- **Date of Oath‑Taking:** 21 April 2026
- **Location:** Putrajaya, Malaysia
- **Family Background:** Son of Gan Kim Sing (lawyer) and Tiong Choo.
