# Anwar Ibrahim – Prime Minister of Malaysia

## Overview
- **Position:** Prime Minister of Malaysia (since December 2022)
- **Party:** People's Justice Party (PKR), a component of the Pakatan Harapan (PH) coalition
- **Role:** Head of the federal government, overseeing national policy, administration, and representing Malaysia internationally.

## 2026 Statement on Election Timing
- On **24 April 2026**, Prime Minister **Anwar Ibrahim** indicated that he is **considering calling a general election in the third quarter**, potentially **by October**, as his government evaluates **politically sensitive fuel‑subsidy cuts**. ## 2026 Statement on Fuel Rationing
- On **24 April 2026**, Prime Minister **Anwar Ibrahim** stated that government action had ensured **no fuel rationing** in Malaysia. ## 2026 Statement on Fuel Pricing
- On **24 April 2026**, Prime Minister **Anwar Ibrahim** said the **RON95 price** was **among the lowest in the world**, while it could be as high as **RM10.50 per litre** in other countries.
