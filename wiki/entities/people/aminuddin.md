# Aminuddin Harun – Menteri Besar of Negeri Sembilan

## Overview

- **Position:** Menteri Besar (Chief Minister) of Negeri Sembilan, Malaysia.
- **Party:** People's Justice Party (PKR), component of the Pakatan Harapan (PH) coalition.
- **Tenure:** Assumed office in May 2018.

## Recent Statements (2026)

- On **27 April 2026**, Aminuddin stated that the decision on whether Pakatan Harapan should maintain ties with Barisan Negara (BN) rests with the PH leadership. He emphasized that his priority remains “continuing his duties to the state and governing well.”  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 27 April 2026]
- On **27 April 2026**, Aminuddin called an emergency meeting after all 14 UMNO assemblymen withdrew their support for his administration in Negeri Sembilan, leaving the state government in limbo.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 27 April 2026]
- On **27 April 2026**, reports indicated that the political crisis in Negeri Sembilan was compounded by a bid from four traditional chieftains to remove the Yang di‑Pertuan Besar of the state, heightening tensions between the state government and the monarchy.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 27 April 2026]
