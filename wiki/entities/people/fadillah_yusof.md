# Fadillah Yusof – Deputy Prime Minister of Malaysia

## Overview
- **Position:** Deputy Prime Minister (DPM) of Malaysia (appointed 2023) and Minister of Energy Transition and Water Transformation (since February 2024).
- **Party:** Parti Pesaka Bumiputera Bersatu (PBB), a component of Gabungan Parti Sarawak (GPS). GPS is part of the unity government coalition led by Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim.
- **Role:** First DPM from Sarawak; serves as the second‑most senior member of the federal cabinet, representing Sarawak’s interests and overseeing energy transition policy.

## 2026 Statement on Economic Outlook
- On **24 April 2026**, DPM **Fadillah Yusof** warned that Malaysia is shifting **from energy price shocks to disruptions in oil‑and‑gas‑based supply chains**, which could trigger a **second wave of price hikes** across the country’s economy.  He highlighted the need for proactive measures to mitigate the impact on consumers and businesses.
