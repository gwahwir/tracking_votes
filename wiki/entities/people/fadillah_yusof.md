# Fadillah Yusof – Deputy Prime Minister of Malaysia

## Overview
- **Position:** Deputy Prime Minister (DPM) of Malaysia (appointed 2023) and Minister of Plantation Industries and Commodities.
- **Party:** Parti Pribumi Bersatu Malaysia (Bersatu), a component of the Perikatan Nasional (PN) coalition that participates in the current unity government led by Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim.
- **Role:** Serves as the second‑most senior member of the federal cabinet, representing the coalition’s interests and overseeing national policy coordination.

## 2026 Statement on Economic Outlook
- On **24 April 2026**, DPM **Fadillah Yusof** warned that Malaysia is shifting **from energy price shocks to disruptions in oil‑and‑gas‑based supply chains**, which could trigger a **second wave of price hikes** across the country’s economy.  He highlighted the need for proactive measures to mitigate the impact on consumers and businesses.
