# Antara (NGO)

## Overview

- **Type:** Non‑governmental organization (NGO) operating in Malaysia, involved in zakat collection and distribution.
- **Leadership (as of April 2026):** Deputy Chairman (name not disclosed in source).

## Recent Events

- On **21 April 2026**, **three individuals**, including the **deputy chairman of Antara**, were **nabbed** by authorities on suspicion of **allegedly siphoning RM230 million** of zakat funds. Investigations revealed that the funds had been transferred into the deputy chairman’s **company account** for **investment and personal use**.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 2026-04-21]
- The Malaysian Anti-Corruption Commission (MACC) also seized **18 luxury vehicles**, including **Porsches** and a **Mercedes‑Benz**, and **properties valued at RM11 million** connected to the scandal.  
  [Source: Free Malaysia Today, 2026-04-21]
